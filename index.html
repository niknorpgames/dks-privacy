Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dog Kennel Simulator ("the App", "we", "us") — a mobile game published by an individual developer based in Ukraine — collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App, and explains your privacy rights.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Summary

  • The App does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not operate our own servers and do not receive your save data.
  • The App shows optional rewarded advertisements through third-party advertising partners. These partners collect certain device data (such as your Advertising ID) to serve and measure ads.
  • The App offers optional in-app purchases processed by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details.

2. Information Collected Automatically

2.1 Advertising partners

The App uses the Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) mediation platform to show optional, reward-based video advertisements. Ads may be served by the following advertising partners, each of which may collect and process data as an independent controller under its own privacy policy:

PartnerPrivacy policy
Unity LevelPlay / ironSource (Unity Technologies) unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
Google AdMob (Google LLC) policies.google.com/privacy · How Google uses data in AdMob
Unity Ads (Unity Technologies) unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
Meta Audience Network (Meta Platforms, Inc.) facebook.com/privacy/policy

Data these partners may collect from your device includes:

  • Device and other identifiers — in particular the Google Advertising ID (AAID);
  • Approximate location — a coarse, non-precise location derived from your IP address (country/region level);
  • Ad interaction data — which ads were shown, viewed, completed, or clicked;
  • Technical device information — device model, operating system version, language, network type, and similar diagnostics.

This data is used to serve ads, limit how often you see the same ad, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. Depending on your consent choice (see Section 4), ads may be personalized or non-personalized; non-personalized ads still use limited device data for delivery, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.

2.2 In-app purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (virtual currency packs) processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Google processes your payment under its own privacy policy. We receive only anonymized transaction confirmations (product identifier and transaction ID) needed to deliver your purchase inside the game. We never receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or other payment credentials.

2.3 Crash logs and diagnostics

Basic crash reports and diagnostic data (device model, OS version, crash stack traces) may be collected automatically through Google Play services and the Unity engine to help us identify and fix technical problems.

2.4 Game progress

Your game save (kennel, dogs, in-game currency, settings) is stored locally on your device only. It is not uploaded to us. If you uninstall the App or change devices, your local progress may be lost.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Account credentials (the App has no account system)
  • Precise (GPS) location
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, files, calendar, or messages
  • Health or biometric data

4. Your Choices and Consent

  • Ad consent. On first launch, the App asks for your consent regarding personalized advertising. You can decline; in that case, ads shown to you will be non-personalized.
  • Watching ads is always optional. All advertisements in the App are opt-in rewarded videos — the App never forces you to watch an ad.
  • Reset your Advertising ID. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of ads personalization at any time in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads (or Settings → Privacy → Ads, depending on your device).
  • Purchases are optional. The App is fully playable without any purchase.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we and our partners rely on the following legal bases: your consent (personalized advertising), legitimate interests (non-personalized ad delivery, fraud prevention, crash diagnostics, securing and improving the App), and performance of a contract (delivering in-app purchases you request).

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain servers and therefore do not store your personal data ourselves. Data collected by our advertising and platform partners is retained according to their own retention policies (see the links in Section 2.1). Local game data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the GDPR in the EU/EEA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because the personal data described above is collected by our advertising and platform partners rather than stored by us, the fastest way to exercise these rights is usually directly with the relevant partner via the links in Section 2.1. You can also contact us (Section 10) and we will forward your request to the relevant partners and assist where we can.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

9. International Data Transfers

Our advertising and platform partners may process data on servers located outside your country, including the United States. Where required by applicable law, these partners state that they use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) for such transfers — see their privacy policies linked in Section 2.1.

10. Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g. a court or a government agency), or where disclosure is necessary in good faith to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights, prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the App, or protect the personal safety of users or the public. As we do not store your personal data ourselves, any such disclosure would in practice be limited to information available to us at that time (such as support correspondence you have sent us).

11. Security

Data exchanged between the App and our partners' services is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data processed by our partners.
